About Odia Catholic Community Bangalore

Odia Catholic Community Bangalore brings together faithful Catholics to worship, pray, and serve through the sacraments and parish life.

Mission

Support our parish family by sharing mass updates, sacramental preparation, prayer intentions, and service opportunities.

What we offer

  • Mass schedules and communion services
  • Priest announcements and spiritual guidance
  • Prayer requests and parish gatherings
  • Donation drives and community outreach
